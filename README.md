NecrOS G4 isn't just a port; it's an optimization masterclass. Based on **Adélie Linux** and **musl libc**, it delivers a "snappy" experience even on 256MB of RAM.

- **Big Endian Advantage:** Fully optimized for the PPC32 RISC architecture.

## 💾 Installation: "Just Works" (Mostly)
We believe in the "Apple way": simplicity. But with a terminal-first twist.

1. **Base System:** Install **Adélie Linux (PPC32)** on your G4.
2. **Download NecrOS:**
   ```bash
   wget -qO- https://raw.githubusercontent.com/WaD45/NecrOS-G4-alternative/main/install.sh | sh
   ```
3. **Ascend:** Reboot into your new tactical workspace.
